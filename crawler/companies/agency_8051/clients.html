Client list

Isle of Skye Distillers Ltd

Timeframe: Present

Bottle design and packaging design for Misty Isle Gin. we designed the main branding for distillery along with an eCommerce website. We started from scratch the social media strategy and we gained 10,000 genuine likes within 20 weeks. On single social media posts we command 20K+ interactions with our competitions. EW:DM have provided all copywriting and brand keyword and consumer propositions. Product goes live January 2017.

Orkney Tourism Group - marketing

Timeframe: Present

For the 4th year in a row, we have designed, managed and published the official Orkney Tourism guide. We design and write the entire front end which is segmented to visitor types. We also create the back end accommodation listings and adverts. We manage and collate the data from the client and present this in a logical format that is easy for the visitor to book the type of accommodation they require. We liaise with the marketing team at VisitScotland. We produce over 50,000 visitor guides that are then distributed throughout all the UK's visitor information centres.

Sons of Amazon

Timeframe: Present

Sons of Amazon are to launch a coffee brand in the UK in June. It will be the UK's strongest coffee. It will also be ethically sourced. They have identified a single source coffee plantation in the Amazon Rainforest, and the beans will be bought through the Rainforest Alliance. Roasted in Scotland to the maximum caffeine levels and sold within an Amazon.com shop front. EW:DM managed all negotiation to secure a TM and R with Amazon.com and class 30 has been granted. Three products are to be sold, Just the Beans, Dust Grind and Rocky Grind, each developed for different types of coffee machines found at home. All branding, digital, TM negotiations and sales strategy managed by EW:DM.

Coffee Conscience

Timeframe: Present

Every cup sold makes a difference, as a percentage of profits goes direct to local environmental projects. EW:DM has produced all the branding, packaging, cups and advise on strategic marketing including new business opportunities and sectors. Coffee Conscience was set up to develop Fairtrade coffee, tea, and hot chocolate within the colleges and universities of Scotland. Every cup that is purchased, a percentage of profits go back to local environmental community projects. Coffee Conscience is unique in the UK and have become a successful brand within educational establishments. Coffee Conscience recently planted their 1000th fruit tree as part of the ongoing support for the Commonwealth orchards community initiative. They sell over ?1m worth of coffee per year.

Borders Journeys

Timeframe: Present

We provide all the digital strategy, design and implementation of this award winning tour company. One of the very first EW:DM clients, we still look after all of Borders Journeys marketing and communications on and off line. Borders Journeys was set up at the same time as EW:DM and was a meeting of minds in a window less training room when both companies were helped to set up by the Glasgow Go Group. Borders Journeys spotted a gap in the market for a tour operator in the Borders region. They have now successfully expanded and tour across all of Scotland. We often bump into them in Orkney!.

Chisholm Hunter Jewellers

Timeframe: Present

We designed and produced the main 1857 magazine. for this high street jewellery brand. The company is 160 year old in 2017 and we were tasked to produce a magazine that not only showcased the best in their jewellery and diamond ranges, but to highlight the heritage and the future of this brand. We were responsible or the main lifestyle shots which were done in a boutique renovated castle and we even had a rare 1961 E-type Jag as a prop. We negotiated and secured this unique car as a prop and is one of only 70 remaining in the world. All art direction and styling was carried out by the EW:DM team. The magazine will be available in-store throughout the 21 UK outlets as well as an online version.

Orkney Tourism Group - online

Timeframe: Present

We have completed a new custom built CMS website for our long standing client Orkney Tourism Group. The website holds all the information you need for your stay on the Islands and how to get there. It also has individual islands highlighted with a robust 'Things to See and Do' section. We deliver 1.25-1.5 million hits/page views for this year. It has: Over 600 pages. Stunning commissioned location photography. Approx 550 accommodation and service providers listed. An Interactive mapping system. An innovative 'mega menu' design. Enhanced accommodation listings, highlighting facilities and prices. Video integration and linked home page sliders. A fully integrated Events section that is kept up-to-date daily. Fully iPad and mobile compatible. Number one, page one on Google for nominated key search terms.

Hotelier International

Timeframe: Present

Published in over 23 countries. We are the art directors and designers of this global publication. Hotelier International, is a magazine created to inspire and inform hoteliers by showcasing the worlds BEST hotels. We aim to lead and enlighten through thought provoking articles and highlight the best in hotel accommodation around the world. We set very high design and art direction standards. Hotelier International magazine is deemed the best designed and informative magazine in this sector. It has won awards. The magazine is published quarterly. We print over 200,000 copies and is distributed worldwide. The publication is a franchise business with international regions. The publication is managed from Stockholm, the editor is based in Barcelona, our writers are in New York, and all design is created in Glasgow. A true global collaboration. Readership is approx: 200,000 (print) Published: Quarterly Target Audience: key purchasers, managers hotel and leisure 4/5 star. Tone: Aspirational, authoritative Distribution: Global under licence

Italian Aroma Coffee

Timeframe: Present

EW:DM have produced all the branding, designs, consumer trade packaging, social media and the on-line sales strategy for this highly respected Scottish based coffee brand. Over the last 3-5 years this brand has seen sales increase by over 50%. Italian Aroma Coffee supply trade and retail coffee to a wide range of independent retailers throughout the UK and offer a distributor business model. We are launching new marketing materials to the trade and further highlighting and expanding the brand on a national basis. Based in Clydebank, they roast, pack, and sell over 6 tonnes of fresh coffee per week to restaurants, cafes, and hotel chains throughout the UK, and Europe. Eric Witham, has been the marketing consultant for Italian Aroma Coffee for over 15 years overseeing the brand expansion and introducing new retail products and initiatives. Italian Aroma Coffee has a yearly turnover of 2.5m+

Glenmorangie

Timeframe: Present

Barry Grove is internationally regarded as one of the finest large stone sculptors working today.Barry has been carving large stone projects for over 20 years and since graduating with a BA(Hons) in Design from Dundee Art College in 1989 has been specialising in traditional Scottish carving. He has worked closely with Historic Scotland and respected experts in this field on a variety of projects over the years as well as accepting numerous private commissions which includes commissions by the Royal family, large international corporations and high-net-worth private clients. Barry works closely with Glenmorangie and was commissioned to reproduce the Hilton of Cadboll Stone, a project that took over 4 years to complete. Barry was also commissioned to produce 'Reveal' a 25 tone stone sculpture for the new production plant for Glenmorangie in Livingston. A project that took over 2 years to complete! Barry is a brand ambassador for Glemnorangie whisky. Eric Witham Design & Marketing has consulted and worked with Barry and Glenmorangie promoting these projects on an international remit. Barry currently lives and works on the Tarbat Ness peninsula, Ross-shire, Scotland.

Balmaha Brewing Company

Timeframe: Present

We've developed the names, branding, packaging and website for one of Scotland's newest micro-breweries.Nestled on the banks of Loch Lomond, this family-run craft brewery sells a range of artisan ales including Lomond Hop, Balmaha Best, and Firkin Point. We wanted to retain the crafted, bespoke tradition of the ales, but also make it relevant for the ale drinker of today. Using a combination of bespoke illustration, a carefully chosen colour palette and bold fonts, each bottle tells the Balmaha story.

Twinings Tea

Timeframe: Past

Design and implemented sales promotional activity for the flavoured black tea range across the UK and European markets. Created all hero imagery and brand assets.

Energizer Batteries

Timeframe: Past

Energizer e2 is a new type of long lasting battery for modern devices on the move. Eric Witham designed the branding utalising the main Energizer logo and giving an unique brand personality. We created all packaging designs for all the range of battery types from AA, AAA to specialist camera batteries. All our packaging designs are adapted for all territories globally. We created an innovative way of using the cardboard sleeve to hold the PET plastic containing the batteries. And this solution allows for easy opening and avoiding 'dropping' or spilling the batteries which could easily then be lost. We also created an unique set of icons that transcends all language barriers. This packaging sits well in the premium battery market and since launch continues to dominate the premium, long-lasting battery sector.

GlaxoSmithKline

Timeframe: Past

GlaxoSmithKline is the largest pharmaceutical company in the world. Eric Witham a few years ago was a retained consultant on the Zovirax lip balm product where we were asked to support the above-the-line activity through brand and consumer engagement. We designed the packaging for the new pump product, various information leaflets, website, various POS items and we successfully distributed over 1 million, handbag sized 'z-card' information leaflets. We ran various promotional in-store activities, designed counter-top display units and ensured the consumer has the right information in combating common lip 'cold sores'.

Lloyds - Living the Dream magazine

Timeframe: Past

Lloyds Living the Dream magazine is the ultimate luxury lifestyle magazine for the affluent and discerning.The magazine reflects and showcases the best in luxury living, from homes and travel, fashion and trends, motoring and boating, capturing all that's aspirational and must-have for an exclusive, high net-worth readership. Readership: 15,000 (print, plus additional online readers) Published: Quarterly. Target Audience: High net worth individuals. Tone: Aspirational, epitomising the best that money can buy. Distribution: Estate agency, select mailing list, 5 star hotels worldwide Key Successes: We implemented a shift change from a local to an international focus. Distribution set up to include Russia and Dubai. Creating a magazine that is truly a 'coffee table' publication. Agency responsible for all budgetary control and risk. Bringing on board key advertisers such as Harrods and Lamborghini. Upgrading the editorial to include 'A' list stars. Created an unique online advertisers directory through the website.
