Client list

Gaggenau

Timeframe: Present

Lime is the lead creative agency for this prestigious German kitchen brand, part of the Bosch Siemens family. After completing a full strategic audit and review of all existing trade and consumer marketing collateral, Lime are developing the brand, all marketing trade and consumer collateral for offline and online, as well as future planning anniversary and special event projects for 2016 and beyond. Lime will be setting up a new PR division in response to the clients need for this role to be central and work closely with the brand development produced by Lime.

Stena Line Ferries

Timeframe: Present

Originally commissioned to create a series of engaging and upbeat e-shots to drive traffic and custom on their Harwich to Hook of Holland route (which saw an 89% increase in bookings), Lime has since been engaged to significantly raise the entire online profile of Stena routes across the UK, Ireland, France and Holland. Lime has not only developed, and continues to produce, e-marketing support across all products, we have developed a language and tone of voice that has been adapted company wide. Lime also provide creative support across other medias that include TV, radio, outdoor, press, PR, direct mail, social media and internal staff communications, ensuring Stena has strong imagery and powerful copy that engages their audience instantly and effectively. Lime also audited the Swedish customer journey process and developed new creative marketing templates for all online activity.

Cass Business School

Timeframe: Present

Lime recently completed the 2016 visual identity reband for Cass Business School. This included producing a new corporate look and feel to sit with City, University of London, then apply sub-branding for each of the departments to sit under the new brand. Marketing items were developed which also formed part of a the new brand guidelines produced by Lime. Items looked at digital and print comms, brochures, event items, online banners and internal publications/items. Lime also commissioned and directed photography to capture iconic and unusual London images, taken from the ground and via helicopter as part of the VI. Lime will continue to develop and produce marketing items going forward.

The Royal British Legion

Timeframe: Present

Ongoing design and production of menus, brochures, programmes and invites for a series of events including The Festival of Remembrance, St George's Day Celebrations and Christmas Concert & Dinner for this worthwhile national charity. Lime has also been working to produce an annual Special Events Brochure that highlights the Legions star events to promote awareness and raise funding.

The Financial Times

Timeframe: Present

Working with the in-house acquisitions team to create stunning campaigns that drive up subscription numbers, build awareness and increase audience engagement.

City University

Timeframe: Present

E-banner campaign to drive traffic. The new reality of academia having to sell itself to students globally, City University chose Lime to propel them to the fore online. The Arts and Social Sciences School needed to appeal to UK and international students who increasingly view higher education as an investment. Our banner campaigns carried all the necessary course information, but utilised results-driven images, assuring students that they were buying more than an education, they were starting their high-flying career. Lime are working on a Donor Report that includes copywriting, design, artwork and print delivery for the fundraising deparment, for both Cass and City.

Gill Jennings & Every LLP

Timeframe: Present

Lime have been developing a suite of marketing collateral to support their global new business drive.

British Cardiovascular Society

Timeframe: Present

Brand refresh, design for print, digital - Lime was brought in to freshen up the BCS brand. This has been done gradually across a number of printed and online elements from conference leaflets, posters, event programmes, web buttons, pop up banners, specialist press advertising, training course flyers and their annual report. In particular the Annual Conference and Exhibition Programme, a 120 page pocket sized book, has been designed, printed and delivered for the same budget year on year, whilst adding greater functionality and a developed look and feel. Lime continue to work with BCS on an ongoing basis producing their annual marketing and event collateral.

Lloyd's of London

Timeframe: Present

Lime's digital team developed and built an engaging animation to communicate effectively to Lloyd's clients, partners, suppliers and employees exactly what it is they do. We continue to support the Lloyd's marketing team, producing printed and electronic reports, internal campaigns/collateral, event materials and online banner advertising.

Institute for Sustainability

Timeframe: Present

Brand origination, annual reports, website, Retrofit Guides - a range of online and offline marketing materials for a targeted audience. Lime originated the brand for this world-class centre for research excellence into sustainable technologies. IfS has brought together industry and academic leaders to create a central hub of expertise that focuses on practical and commercial innovations that reduce environmental impact. The 'gem' brand represents the Institute's partnering of practical need with expertise. It communicates an ever-evolving, connected and supportive centre for a better future. Lime continues to work with the Institute to develop their marketing and creative strategy as they move forward.

Alison Price

Timeframe: Present

Lime are working closely with the team at Alison Price on a website redesign, creating a high-end site to reflect their unique offering.

GSMA (mobile technologies)

Timeframe: Present

Stand-out event collateral, Smart Cities Index microsite, pull-out business cards and new product event apps. Developing a targeted, effective piece of stand-out event collateral for one area of the global GSMA mobile operator business - mEducation, Lime were right on the money. A complimentary pull-out business card holder was added for three areas of their business including Connected Living. Additionally GSMA then commissioned a full size A4 brochure for the mAutomotive Connected Car Forum that conveyed a wide range of messages in a conventional but visually high impact printed piece. Based on the warm reception to all Lime designs to date, we are currently working on several online and offline projects for them, including the recently developed mAutomotive app for global events, event and marketing materials for Capacity Building division, and white paper reports for government. A lot of the items Lime produce are also adapted for Chinese and Arabic markets.

Timeframe: Present

Brand development, direct mail and advertising - BFRC wanted a professional, credible and modern brand that reflected their reputable status in the energy rating community. Lime's creative team came up with a complete brand re-design to fit this brief along with a complementary set of marketing collateral including trade press ads, van graphics, event handouts, flyers and corporate certificates. Lime continue to work with BFRC on an ongoing basis as their key creative agency.

Lime are also looking at a redesign and SEO functionality for their current website.

Harp Wallen - Executive Recruitment

A name synonymous with the ultimate in luxury and service for over 80 years. The then Orient-Express marketing team have benefitted from lime's marketing creativity that successfully sells them to both consumer and business sectors. As well as selling their unique packages for 5 star gourmet and cultural experiences, for over 5 years Lime produced effective direct mail, brochures, on-line promotional ideas and event material creating maximum impact where responses and sales have always exceeded expectations and award-winning collateral. Vascular Society

Timeframe: Past

Brand creation and roll-out across all areas of marketing. Membership association website build, annual reports and creative support. Together with a rebrand for this leading charity, the digital team here at Lime also completely rebuilt the new Vascular Society website increasing functionality and content with a membership platform and improved usability. Lime also developed an app for attendees to their annual AGM which was downloaded via the Apple store and included all event sessions, speakers and presentation titles, with live unique twitter feeds for attendee interaction and discussion.

Sustainable Industries Park

Timeframe: Past

Originally controlled under the London Thames Gateways Development Corporation's funding, and now with the Greater London Authority (GLA), Lime was involved in the brand origination, website build and production of a wide range of online and offline marketing support/collateral. Lime originated the brand with strategic underpinning, we knew we needed to reflect that this was a new dimension of industries park, one of the UK's largest concentration of environmental industries and technologies at the historic Dagenham Dock. The brand needed to communicate that SIP is a focal point, very well connected, with major transport links providing good access to materials, markets and workforce. This brand with its shoots & networks intersecting in the box, describes the way like-minded businesses are brought together on one site to support the development of new technologies, supply renewable energy and also recycle and process waste. Lime continue to provide marketing support to the GLA.

Lime has also rebranded DWSN from it's previous name, developing a new creative look and feel to suppor the name change.

London Thames Gateway Development Corporation

Timeframe: Past

Website, annual reports, billboard campaigns and development of the award-winning East London: Hotspots campaign for the promotion of this area of London. Bold, colourful graphics were devised to grab attention, challenge preconceptions and highlight the extensive potential of the area.
