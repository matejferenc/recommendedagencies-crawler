Client list

the happy egg co. USA

Timeframe: Present

In 2015, Blimey helped bring their Hendependence campaign to life which included the campaign identity, microsite, social media content, unique print with 3D pop-up effects as well as their Farm scene exhibition stand which captured what True Free Range is all about. The stand was designed and built in the UK, and then transported to the US. We even sent our own Blimey exhibition crew to assemble and be on hand for Expo West in LA. The piece de resistance was a stop motion video, made in collaboration with A + C and ex-Aardman model maker Jim Parkin. The concept was created from scratch in the Blimey office and has been used across social media channels and digital banners with a great response from fans.

the happy egg co. USA

Timeframe: Present

Since 2012 Blimey has continued to support with their launch of the brand in the US. Working closely with happy egg's US advisors we expanded our UK work offering for an American audience - and they are now officially the no.1 free-range brand on the egg aisle. This is one brand where strategic implementation and creative tools are essential to compete for shelf space. As well as a whole range of POS, Blimey created some novel pieces for their PR campaign launch to the trade and media. First, a video box with a mini video screen playing a happy eggs promo. Second, our 'free-range farm in a box', a micro-detailed and transportable case opened up to show exactly what happy eggs mean by free range.

Orsom Handmade Cheese

Timeframe: Present

Blimey pulled out all the stops when supporting the launch of this exciting new brand of cheese. As fitting for a 'handmade cheese with a touch of personality', we created a brand and identity rich in character - Winston, Grace, Woodew and Orsom Blue. Individual packaging for each of the four cheeses - plus a starring role in a fun-filled photo-shoot at Woodborough Hall - allowed each cheese to show off its character to the full. Launched through consumer shows, festivals - including Carfest and the Nantwich Cheese Festival - and an e-commerce website (in customised magento), Orsom Cheese is going from strength to strength. Seeing Grace, a delicate English white cheese, winning a national award just weeks after launch was a highlight.

Fox's Biscuits

Timeframe: Past

Launching new products can be a stressful time. But when it comes to the crunch, Blimey Creative always delivers. Working in partnership with London's Mother and Bloom, we created a huge variety of material to launch a new brand for Fox's Biscuits in Morrisons, including posters, shelf talkers and 3D floor graphics. Similarly, Rocky Big Eat got the big Blimey treatment with eye-catching POS in Asda and Morrisons, backed by trade advertising for wholesaler Bestway and outdoor media on cricket perimeter boards. Blimey worked on new products including festive favourites, a party ring tube and a Mini Vinnie tube featuring the character familiar from TV ads. And reflecting the trust Fox's has in Blimey, we were given a completely open brief as sole agency on the Party Princess brand.

Noble Foods

Timeframe: Present

Noble Foods is the UK's leading supplier of fresh eggs to major retailers, and responsible for the UK's most familiar egg brands. They rely on Blimey Creative for compelling internal communications to ensure the industry take notice of this category and their customers appreciate their knowledge and dedication to supplying the consumer market with a choice on the egg aisle. Blimey work behind the food industry scenes in supporting their category and sales teams deliver their objectives and targets. We always offer a fresh creative approach to traditional B2B communications pieces. From internal corporate magazines to trade exhibition stands, this is one business that is progressive and dynamic in its approach to marketing, which is why we think creatively in our approach to developing solutions that elevate and match their business profile. We take care of two glossy and informative internal magazines for them - Egg Express and Noble Today - as well as trade external exhibition material. More recently, where we have really come into our own, is looking after the Noble Foods Conference in establishing the conference theme, a video and PPT. But we all know it's the detail that counts, and no detail was too small for the team at Blimey. We carried the theme onto the tables, badges and leaflets.

The Resort Group plc

Timeframe: Present

One of Europe's leading international property developers, the Resort Group specialises in world-class resorts and hotels that provide first-class investment returns for their clients. Blimey has helped promote developments on the islands of Cape Verde, 350 miles off the coast of West Africa. As well as designing brand identities for each of their resorts we get to work closely behind the scenes ensuring their own corporate identity remains fresh and current when sitting among the sales collateral. From advertising and e-shots, design and build of their corporate website to the complex development of their online application, PIF, Property Investment Finder, we have experience working across a number of projects. The Resort Group is a progressive business in the world of property investment and we need to think creatively as well as dynamically with this one. And as for exhibitions, well that's a story all of its own! As a client The Resort Group's investor products are a little different as they don't sit on a shelf and require more strategic approach with solid grounding, check out the latest development in their portfolio, White Sands. With the expansion of The Resort Group's property portfolio in Cape Verde, we were challenged to top our previous creative work once again. To us, creating a brand identity and prestigious brochure for the Boa Vista Island's White Sands Hotel and Spa, was an opportunity to experiment with print. This needed a unique style. So, as well as a series of stunning shots, and with the help of local specialist Nottingham printer Chameleon, we gave the brochure front cover a textured and highly tactile white finish which feels just like stroking the pure white sands of the island. The creative team sourced a variety of different cover options, finally selecting a flock effect from Scotland that captured exactly the unique texture we wanted. The identity sits proudly in the Resort Group portfolio and we look forward to working on the next one. Finally, as part of our ongoing dedication to digital marketing, we have more recently worked with The Resort Group in developing a content marketing strategy and helped to generate insightful and useful content, such as infographics, that help showcase their knowledge and leadership positioning online in the world of investment.

Delilah Fine Foods Ltd

Timeframe: Present

The dedicated hard-core foodies at Delilah know a tasty proposition when they see one. And that's exactly what they got when they chose Blimey to provide its lovely logo and livery all those years ago. A good design is one thing but a great design stands the test of time and that's just what we delivered. For nearly 10 years, the creative team has helped deliver strategic marketing support and advice that now places Delilah as the number one award winning deli in Nottingham. Dedicated to bringing both new products and variety to their loyal customers, their branding needs to live up to the quality that the consumers have grown to expect. Blimey also helped create and bring Delilah's very own branded range to the shelf and trust us when we say the rose-infused chocolate is to die for. To round it off we launched their first e-commerce platform which will now offer their long distance customer base the opportunity to buy whenever they want.

The Happy Egg Co.

Timeframe: Present

The happy egg co. brand team keep us on our toes, which you have to expect from such a fun brand - we even got to drive a bus! Working closely with the brand's specialist agencies, the Blimey team helped bring their experiential tour plans to life. From design and build to even sourcing a mechanic, Blimey have created some of the most engaging and fun materials for a campaign that is bound to win awards. As part of their dedication to education strategy, the happy egg co. asked Blimey to help create an experience for consumers and schools that would enlighten and educate in a fun way, that only happy egg as a brand can deliver. From chicken skeletons to dinosaur heads, our creative team surpassed themselves on this one!

Samworth Brothers

Timeframe: Present

The Samworth Group enlisted our help in creating a backdrop of interiors that turned an event into a "Heston" style experience for their clients. With only days to prepare for their dignitaries visit to the factory, the team at Blimey translated their brief quickly and transformed an area within their offices to a one-off fine dining restaurant at night to a contemporary deli style kitchen/coffee bar the next day. The 24-hour project included design, photography and a little sprinkling of Blimey dust to demonstrate how dedicated Walkers are to delivering a customer-centric approach with their NPD, and showing just what true quality is. Since this VIP visit Blimey are now working behind the scenes regularly with one of Britain's great leaders in the food industry. It wasn't long until another perfect opportunity arose, and the creative team was more than happy to get their teeth into "National Ham Week". The main objective for the fine deli connoisseur was not just to retain their shelf space but to expand their category offering with this national retailer too. Along came Blimey, with a strategy, campaign and concepts to help them achieve their goal on the day. It was a roaring success - with the current listing retained and shelf space secured, our presentation also culminated in a category extension for Walkers Deli.

eatlean Protein Cheese

Timeframe: Present

Launching a new product is always an exciting process, especially now that social media can make a huge impact on your following. For eatlean cheese, a new Protein product to hit the market this year, we strategically planned the marketing campaign to work with the rollout and launch across three major UK supermarkets. Starting from scratch can be difficult but we love a challenge at Blimey so we set to work creating and maintaining an ongoing online and social media presence. Concentrating on three major channels - Twitter, Facebook and Instagram - meant we could carefully tailor content to the target market. Alongside this, development and design of the website allowed for clear communication with the audience. We took creative direction of the bespoke product photography to display practical use of the cheese through delicious-looking meals, while our management and direction of the photo shoot with Brand Ambassador, England and Northampton rugby player, Ben Foden, helped to boost the brand. Reaching out to social media influencers and bloggers, as well as industry and national press outlets, gave the ultimate 360 coverage, and we ensured this continued with ongoing interaction to build community and brand loyalty. All of which combined to leave consumers in no doubt which protein product they wanted in their fridges.
