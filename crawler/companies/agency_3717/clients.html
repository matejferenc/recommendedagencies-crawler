The Co-operative Group

Timeframe: Past

The Co-operative Green Schools Revolution iPhone game, is an app developed by the team at Delineo and the third iPhone application released by The Co-operative Group. Aimed at school children in primary and secondary education, the app features a number of exciting mini games designed to teach young minds about green issues. Players can protect a field of carrots from ravenous rabbits, save electricity in a room full of light bulbs and save a rubber duck from the rising tides by turning off taps. The game, which you can download here, was produced in conjunction with our branding and advertising work with the Green Schools Revolution education programme. Speaking about the app, Melanie Phillips, who head up The Co-operative's Green Schools Revolution programme, said: "We are lucky that our youngsters have such a tremendous appetite to learn more about green issues. Our new Green Schools Revolution app is a fun and engaging way to help children and young people learn about building a more sustainable world whilst competing with their friends and family to become eco warriors!"

Ecowater

Timeframe: Present

The world's largest manufacturer of household water softeners and drinking water filters has launched its 'The Joy of Ecowater' consumer marketing campaign. Manchester marketing agency Delineo was tasked to create an engaging consumer campaign that highlights the benefits of having an Ecowater system in the home and the benefits of soft water to the family. The key message "The Joy of Ecowater" is supported by a number of different executions that show each member of a family enjoying water in different ways. Each execution highlights a benefit of soft water for that person. Whether it's a baby in bath, where softer water is kinder to their skin or simply using the dishwasher, knowing softer water will stop lime scale build up. The friendly imagery and reassuring tone of voice embraces everything about experiencing "The Joy of Ecowater". The campaign integrated web, press ads, video as well as a full suite of marketing collateral to support the campaign.

Soundnet

Timeframe: Past

Soundnet a leading supplier of music and video content tasked Delineo to develop a digital campaign for its Background Music System business. The aim of the campaign is to attract traffic and generate leads from businesses looking for a legal and affordable music solution. The campaign targets pub, bar, shop, restaurants, caf? and hotel operators, emphasising if they get the right background music in their premises, customers will spend more time with them as their experience will be enhanced. The key benefits of the Soundnet Background music system is that it's easily installed into any PC (requiring only a low spec) and Soundnet's team of music profile experts will formulate a playlist tailored to suit the venue and customer type. It can be updated monthly and can be set to create different atmospheres at different times of the day. Many premises often use MP3 libraries such as iTunes or Spotify without realising that these services do not grant the commercial use of their music, consequently operators are in breach of copyright law - Soundnet can guarantee a digital background music service that provides a fully legal solution. Through extensive research of the most searched for terms and phrases in relation to background music systems for businesses and a full competitor review, Delineo identified the most relevant target audience for Soundnet in order to structure the website content and create the most cost effective PPC campaign. Delineo refreshed the whole background music section, improving visitor navigation, strengthening call to actions, adding blogs, incorporating imagery and created fully optimised business specific landing pages (e.g. bars, hotels) to support the targeted PPC campaign. It was clear early on into the campaign that the right people were being directed to the site without Soundnet having to invest too heavily on Adwords, and those who made contact with Soundnet typically were the ideal prospect and conversion to sale has been impressive. Within a 6 month timeframe visitors to the website have increased by 200%, with organic rankings significantly improving and searches yielding a 150% increase. In response to the campaign, Soundnet's Commercial Director said: 'The SEO and PPC campaign has generated excellent leads for Soundnet, great work Delineo'.

On Delineo's appointment, ICM's Head of Marketing said: "Thanks for your teams work on this. We have had lots of positive feedback about the rebrand and ad campaign''.

The Co-operative Travel

Timeframe: Past

After a successful 5-way pitch, two issues of the internal comms magazine 'Shine' have been released to over 3,0000 Co-operative Travel staff. The Co-operative Travel Internal Communications Manager said: 'Off all the design agencies who tendered for the relaunch of the in-house magazine Shine, Delineo's proposal was easily the most radical and the most interesting. They rethought our communications from the ground up, taking a holistic approach while always focusing on the end user. That's why they were successful and I'm looking forward to the relaunched magazine's publication.' In response to the magazine's makeover, The Co-operative Travel Internal Communications Manager said: 'Shine has been transformed from an employee magazine that was easy to leave on the desk into a publication that looks like Glamour, contains more content in more pages, and has definitely made a big impact on its audience.'

The Co-operative Bank

Timeframe: Present

Bank re-launch Planning rationale: Over the last 18 months, The Co-operative Bank has been through the most difficult period in its history. The well documented issues have led them to a point where their brand health was at an all-time low meaning they needed to do something big. Arc London has been working on the positioning of the Bank for the last nine months and has developed a brand positioning 'banking with a conscience'. The resulting advertising campaign includes striking imagery aimed to reinforce their position as an ethical alternative based on their customer led Ethical Policy. Delineo's brief was to develop and amplify the campaign in the digital space, pick up the story where the ATL campaign left off, to bring it to life so the positioning is believable. Creative outcome: Working together with a number of other agencies, including Arc London, iProspect and the digital & marketing team's at the Bank - Delineo initially developed the Hub. This is a central point where a lot of the campaign comms. point towards to, to provide information on the proof points behind the campaign. The key was to 'extend' the story of the ATL campaign and give a rich content hub where relevant content would be used to tell our story. As the campaign evolves so will the Hub with more content added to provide further engagement. Together with the Hub, Delineo produced a number of display ads in various formats, which were used across a high profile of networks (with an opportunity to be seen over 150 million times over a 3 month period) to further bring to life the campaign's key messages. Social media and content marketing is also being used to further extend the messages and provide content which reinforces the proposition. Delineo are working on producing video content in the form of real life customer examples, interactive games as well as infographics and interactive maps, which highlight the positive impact The Co-operative Bank has made across the UK.

Azlan

Timeframe: Present

Multinational Website PLANNING RATIONALE: Azlan needed our help to bring to life a groundbreaking joint initiative between itself and HP. Pitched as the most innovative channel initiative and partner program HP Europe has ever seen, Azlan wanted it to be presented as a focused, resource-rich fast-track to stronger revenues. Straight away, we decided that our messaging had to get straight to the point. Sales teams in IT channel organisations are generally profit-driven, hugely focused, and work across myriad national cultures. To break into their mindset, we had to build something powerful and distinctive. CREATIVE OUTCOME: A single overarching proposition was used to make an engaging appeal to both Azlan's internal HP teams, and to sales teams working in thousands of partner organisations across 16 European countries. By considering questions like "What's in it for me?" and "What's in it for my customer", we provided immediate access to the business arguments that the sales professionals could take forward to their customers. Because of its extensive functionality, we then selected the EPiServer platform to build the website. Although the translation element of the process was challenging due to the technical nature of the industry, the intuitive new build automatically recognised the country and language of the website visitor to display the correct version of the home page. And, if a partner who worked and travelled internationally logged into the site, it would instantly display the correct home page and language for their nationality. Although the Azlan website was our first build using EPiServer, we were thrilled to be named Partner of the Year as a result of our work. We also delivered a complete suite of brand, online and sales support tools. Previously, nothing of this kind existed, but we created a strong, easily referenced personality for the programme. This enabled Azlan, HP and thousands of business partners to engage with, discuss and get passionate about the initiative.

WS Travel

Timeframe: Present

WST Travel is the fastest growing school travel operator in the UK. In order to capitalise on its growing appeal and demonstrate its unique offering, we took charge of its website development, including UX design, search and social content development, and community outreach. PLANNING RATIONALE: Taking the website from a content-heavy, poor user journey, our development reflected WST's level of expertise in its field. It showed the detailed knowledge that WST specialists bring to planning a school trip, whilst ensuring the user was not overwhelmed by information. CREATIVE OUTCOME We optimised the website to drive users from search engines, and our UX designers and information architects developed a journey focused on driving online conversions. We also created a unique way of searching so that users could view the trips on offer by location, by subject, or by searching for both elements in conjunction with each other. We also put together a handy hints and tips page, location blogs and a live chat feature, in order to provide a level of comfort to parents. The fully responsive functionality meant that the website could be easily accessed on a range of devices by both teachers and parents. The site's successes have been numerous. Crucially, the bounce rate is declining significantly, while sessions, unique visits and users, and page views have all increased.

Manchester Jazz Festival

Timeframe: Present

Social Media Campaign PLANNING RATIONALE: Now in the 19th year, Manchester Jazz Festival is a well-established event on the Manchester social calendar, and although they have enjoyed success in previous years, they wanted to engage with a wider local audience in 2014. In order to connect at a personal and organic level with musicians, sponsors and festival goers, Manchester Jazz festival required a unique campaign that was able to harness a core community of online followers. CREATIVE OUTCOME: Delineo implemented a two month, strategic, social media campaign that used effective creative content across a range of platforms including Facebook, Twitter, Blogs and Vines. This versatile and all-encompassing approach was able to provide followers with a real flavour of the Manchester Jazz Festival. The use of clever key word engagement meant Manchester Jazz Festival was able to harness their core online community. Over 10.2 million tweets were viewed and the homepage visits from social media increased by 113% from the previous year. The unique blend of platforms built enthusiasm and anticipation for the festival, making it a must see event with a wider audience than ever before.

Certas

Timeframe: Present

Agricultural Lubricants DM Concepts PLANNING RATIONALE: Certas' agricultural customers rely heavily on their machinery - it's absolutely vital to keep their farm moving and commercially active, not to mention full utilisation and maintenance of their most expensive assets. Lubricants help to keep machinery moving and working at its best, reducing wear and tear and boosting engine performance. After harvest time was considered to be the perfect opportunity to let customers know that they can get high quality, branded lubricants from their trusted fuel supplier. Harvest would have taken an immense amount of hard work, round the clock long hours and effort, and taken its toll on their machinery. CREATIVE OUTCOME: Delineo's aim was to convert existing agri-fuel buyers, to dual product buyers by developing a tactical direct mail piece to promote buying lubricants from their fuel supplier with an incentive to 'buy now'. The master campaign was then rolled out as two separate brands and styles to account for a premium brand style and a budget alternative whilst remaining immediate and price-led. The concepts produced by the creative team at Delineo were then used as a base for the campaign and range of deliverables. To date, the campaign has produced an eye watering uplift in sales of 1,000 per cent!
